Context

The Project

A Fortune 500 company was migrating critical infrastructure to Azure across multiple global business units. I supported the QA and data migration workstreams, coordinating across an 18-person development team spanning NA, EMEA, and APAC.

My Role

What I Did

1

Mapped the Data Flow System

Understood the end-to-end data flow, identified checkpoints, and documented the process so the team had a clear picture of how data moved through the migration pipeline.

2

Built Reusable Migration Templates

Created standardized templates that streamlined each client's data migration. Each template could be customized with minor adjustments based on client-specific needs, saving time across engagements.

3

Created a Personalized PM Tracker

Built a custom Excel-based tracker to understand and monitor day-to-day progress, giving me and the team better visibility into where things stood at any moment.

4

Facilitated Agile Ceremonies & JIRA Management

Coordinated daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives. Managed 50+ stories per sprint on the JIRA board and added enhancements to improve workflow visibility.

Go-Live Coordination

The go-live was a 2-day process. Before we started, I mapped out every potential blocker we might hit and what we'd do if it happened. We had a blocker meeting protocol ready, response plans documented, and nothing was a surprise. When issues came up, we already knew the playbook.
