Capstone Project

IT PMO Framework Design

Designed a comprehensive IT Portfolio Management Office framework for Alnylam Pharmaceuticals, standardizing how IT projects flow from intake to deployment.

Client Alnylam Pharmaceuticals
Duration 12 Weeks
Team Size 5 Members
My Role Project Lead & PM Coordinator

The Problem

  • No standardized project intake process
  • Unclear decision-making authority across IT
  • Projects starting without proper governance approval
  • Inconsistent prioritization leading to resource conflicts
  • No quality checkpoints between project phases

The Solution

  • Standardized intake form capturing 15+ evaluation criteria
  • Clear RACI matrix defining accountability at every stage
  • 5-stage gate framework with entry/exit criteria
  • Governance structure aligned with business priorities
  • Pilot-tested with sample projects for validation
